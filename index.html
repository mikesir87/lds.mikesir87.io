LDS Gizmos

This is a simple site that contains various things to help out with Family Home Evenings (FHE), lessons, etc.

  • Parable of the Ten Virgins - a simple little app that lets you choose whether to be a wise or not-wise virgin (do you have enough oil to refill your lamp or not?)
  • Countdown - a simple countdown timer that can be used in an online stream. Simply put it as a window capture and add a chromakey filter to make it transparent (why it has a bright green background)
